Counsel Amber Qureshi
Date March 15, 2025

Center for Immigration Law and Policy v ICE (2024–)

Request Data on ICE removals, Secure Communities removals, detentions, arrest/apprehensions, encounters, alternatives to detention, and orders of supervision with anonymized identifiers for immigrants and linked immigration court EOIR case numbers.
Agency Immigration and Customs Enforcement
Requestor UCLA Center for Immigration Law and Policy; David Hausman
Counsel Michael Levin-Gesundheit
Date May 2024
Agency response None
Litigation • Federal district court lawsuit (ongoing)
• Initial data provided March 2025
• Second data release in early June 2025
• Third release in late June 2025
• Fourth release in late July 2025

ACLU v ICE (2018–2023)

The decision in this case led to the requirement that agencies replace private identifiers with anonymized unique IDs if those private identifiers are needed to link different parts of an agency’s database.

Request Data on ICE initial apprehensions, risk classification assessments, detentions, and removals with anonymized identifiers for immigrants.
Agency Immigration and Customs Enforcement
Requestor ACLU Immigrants’ Rights Project; David Hausman
Date Oct. 2018
Agency response None
Litigation • Federal district court lawsuit
• Second Circuit Court of Appeals decision
• Data provided November 2023
