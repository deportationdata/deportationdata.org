## Standardization Changes Made

### 1. CBP Agency Name (Fixed in 6 files)
- **Before**: "Patrulla de Aduanas y Fronteras" (incorrect translation)
- **After**: "Oficina de Aduanas y Protección Fronteriza" (official CBP Spanish name)
- **Source**: Official CBP website and government documents

### 2. Detainer Terminology (Standardized across files)
- **Before**: Mixed usage of "solicitudes de retención" and "órdenes de detención"  
- **After**: Consistent use of "órdenes de detención"
- **Source**: ICE official terminology for immigration detainers

### 3. English Source Corrections
- **Fixed**: "Customs and Border Patrol" → "Customs and Border Protection" in source files
- **Reason**: Corrected the official agency name in English

## Verification Sources

All terms verified against official U.S. government websites including:
- ICE.gov (Spanish sections)

## Usage Guidelines

1. **Consistency**: Use only the standardized terms from this table
2. **Context**: Some terms like "deportación" vs "expulsión" depend on legal context
3. **Updates**: Verify with government sources when new terms are introduced
4. **Documentation**: Always cite government sources for new technical translations
